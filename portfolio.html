-About Amanda-

Hello World,I am an aspiring Frontend Developer.I began my journey into web development in 2020, after spending ten years working in the culinary industry. Earning my Associates of Science in Web Development in 2023 I have since developed a passion for creating fresh designs and concepts to bring to the industry. My inspirations come from retro 60's aesthetics, to more modern applications, with bold color palettes.

My skills from the culinary world I believe give me a new perspective on design and the process of building web applications. Merging my skills in problem solving, time management, teamwork, creative solutions, and artistic expression give me a new spin on what it means to create fun web environments.
